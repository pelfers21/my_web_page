Why I like dogs.

  1. They are cute.
  2. They are good company.
  3. They are fun to play with.

Actually, dogs are pretty smart. They can learn a lot of tricks and are very playful. You must have the patience to teach them, but they are very trainable. I have had many dogs of all different sizes. I prefer small dogs that do not shed their hair. They are very expensive to have groomed regularly, but they do not shed as bad as short-haired dogs. They are also less allergenic for those people that allergic to dogs.
